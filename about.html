who we are

We are a creative, full-service web development training center located in Buea. We train Cameroonians on highly-paid in-demand IT skills to enable them access high quality jobs worldwide. We do not only train young Cameroonians but we also assist our students get jobs upon completion of the program.

When students complete our regular twelve months(9 months fast track) program, we enroll them into the microverse program where they will take another 7 to 11 months to get a highly paid job.

Our main focus is Web Development training but we also render other services such as SEO, wordpress and dital marketing.

We are set on a mission to provide highly paid in-demand IT skills to cameroonians and Africans that will enable them land high quality jobs worldwide. We want to be able to democratize quality technical training making it accessible to the least privileged so that they too can contribute value to society.

To be a leading IT training center in Africa, and most preferred in Cameroon.
